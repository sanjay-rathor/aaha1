    Frequently Asked Questions

    What are the eligibility criteria for this programme?
    Candidates must have completed their 10+2 or equivalent with subjects such as Physics, Chemistry and Biology, securing a minimum of around 50% marks.
    How is the programme structured?
    This 4-year programme is divided into eight semesters with one year of job training. It combines classroom learning with practical training in emergency response, trauma care, pre-hospital services and internships.
    Are scholarships available?
    Yes, Apollo Allied Health Academy provides merit-based and need-based scholarships to students. Candidates can enquire about the scholarships during the admission process.
    What kind of placement support is provided?
    Students receive placement support from our career development cell through mock interviews, resume building, career counselling and placement drives.
    Can I specialise in a specific emergency care field?
    Absolutely, after completing the programme, students can specialise in a specific emergency care field. It includes various areas such as trauma care, disaster management or ambulance services. Students can also pursue higher education.
    Are there opportunities for internships during the course?
    Yes, Apollo Allied Health Academy offers several internship opportunities to students. These opportunities allow students to gain practical exposure and hands-on learning with hospital integration systems such as Apollo Hospitals.
    What is the fee payment schedule?
    The fee payment schedule is simple; students can pay annually or semester-wise. At the time of admission, students can ask about the detailed fee structure or payment timeline.
    What are the higher education opportunities after this programme?
    After completing B.Sc Emergency Medical Technology, students have numerous options for higher studies and specialisations. They can pursue M.Sc. in Emergency & Critical Care and Master of Public Health.
    What is the fee for the programme?
    The fee for B.Sc. Emergency Medical Technology is 2.5 Lakh per annum.
